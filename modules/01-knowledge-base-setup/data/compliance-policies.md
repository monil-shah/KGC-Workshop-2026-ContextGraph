# AFS Compliance & Audit Policies

**Version**: 3.0 | **Last Updated**: 2025-01-10 | **Owner**: AWS Finance / GRC

## 1. Audit Requirements

### 1.1 SOX Compliance

All financial metrics generated by the AFS Integrity Metrics system are subject to SOX compliance requirements:

- Metric outputs must be snapshotted at period boundaries (month-end, quarter-end, year-end)
- Snapshots are immutable once period is closed
- All changes to metric definitions require change management approval
- Audit trail must be retained for 7 years minimum

### 1.2 GRC Controls

| Control ID | Control | Frequency | Owner |
|-----------|---------|-----------|-------|
| GRC-001 | OFA to FASL+ reconciliation variance < $10K | Monthly | FBI CapEx |
| GRC-002 | All POs invoiced within 90 days or escalated | Monthly | Accounting |
| GRC-003 | Asset create success rate > 99.5% | Monthly | AFS Engineering |
| GRC-004 | ERV variance < 5% for 95% of assets | Quarterly | Finance |
| GRC-005 | Full asset inventory reconciliation | Annually | FBI CapEx |

### 1.3 Data Retention

- **Metric outputs**: 7 years (SOX requirement)
- **Transaction-level details**: 7 years
- **Decision traces and audit logs**: 7 years
- **Intermediate calculation data**: 90 days (then archived to S3 Glacier)
- **DataStudio query logs**: 1 year

## 2. Period Close Compliance

### 2.1 Close Calendar

| Period Type | Close Deadline | Metric Refresh Deadline | Certification Deadline |
|------------|---------------|------------------------|----------------------|
| Month-End | Business Day +3 | Business Day +1 | Business Day +2 |
| Quarter-End | Business Day +5 | Business Day +2 | Business Day +4 |
| Year-End | Business Day +10 | Business Day +3 | Business Day +8 |

### 2.2 Escalation Matrix

| Severity | Condition | Response Time | Escalation To |
|----------|-----------|---------------|---------------|
| SEV-1 | Metric generation failure during close | 15 minutes | AFS Engineering + FBI CapEx Lead |
| SEV-2 | Reconciliation variance > $1M | 30 minutes | FBI CapEx Lead + Finance Director |
| SEV-3 | Reconciliation variance > $10K | 4 hours | FBI CapEx Analyst |
| SEV-4 | Minor data quality issue | Next business day | AFS Engineering |

### 2.3 Metric Accuracy Requirements

- Metric framework accuracy: 99.99% (system responsibility)
- Input query accuracy: Owned by business users who define the queries
- Discrepancies between AFS and OFA must be investigated within 48 hours of detection
- All HIGH risk items (> $500K or aging > 120 days) must be reviewed before period close

## 3. Access Control

### 3.1 Data Access

- Metric outputs are available via data lake (Glue Catalog) and shared via OMNI to DataStudio
- Access is controlled at the namespace level
- FBI CapEx, Accounting, and Finance teams have read access to all namespaces
- Write access to metric definitions requires AFS Engineering approval

### 3.2 Change Management

All changes to metric definitions, reconciliation rules, or threshold configurations must follow:

1. Submit change request with business justification
2. Review by AFS Engineering for technical feasibility
3. Review by FBI CapEx for business impact
4. Approval by Finance Director for threshold changes > 20%
5. Implementation with rollback plan
6. Post-implementation verification in next metric refresh

## 4. System Availability

- Metric generation must complete before period close deadlines
- SEV-2 support for metrics framework generation failures
- SEV-2 support for dataset query failures is out of scope (owned by source systems)
- Target availability: 99.9% during close windows, 99.5% otherwise
